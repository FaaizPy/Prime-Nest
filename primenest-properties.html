Property Market
Insights 2024–25

Our research team tracks property trends across Islamabad's key sectors weekly. Here are the key themes shaping the market right now.

01
DHA & Bahria Town Lead Appreciation

Residential plots in DHA Phase 2–4 have appreciated 18–24% over the past 12 months, driven by infrastructure development and restricted supply. Bahria Town follows at 14–17%.

02
Tech Zone Surge in Commercial Demand

STZA-approved zones in Islamabad have seen a 35% increase in commercial leasing enquiries from IT firms. Office space under PKR 250/sqft is now extremely rare in prime zones.

03
Smart Buying Window for New Buyers

With inflation stabilizing and policy rate adjustments anticipated, Q1 2025 represents a strong entry window for end-users. Pre-launch project prices are still 20–30% below completion value.
